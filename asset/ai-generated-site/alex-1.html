About Me

I'm a passionate Media Art + Practice student at USC, exploring the intersection of technology, art, and human experience. My work spans digital installations, interactive media, and experimental design.


Through my studies, I've developed a deep appreciation for how emerging technologies can create meaningful connections and transform traditional artistic practices. I'm particularly interested in immersive experiences and how they can challenge our perception of reality.


When I'm not coding or creating, you can find me exploring LA's vibrant art scene, attending gallery openings, or experimenting with new creative tools.
